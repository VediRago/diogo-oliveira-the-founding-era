# Chapter Two: What Ecalus Had Been

![Rutare Ventari writing his confession](../assets/founding-era-storyboard.jpg)

I never knew the man Father used to be. I knew him only through what others remembered.

A servant saying he used to laugh. Calla in the kitchens, old enough to remember how it was before.

She told me once that Ecalus used to interrupt dinner with his hands.

That was how she said it.

With his hands.
