# Chapter Three: The Sentence I Gave Him

![Rutare Ventari writing his confession](../assets/founding-era-storyboard.jpg)

Something about Ermerus repelled Father.

Ecalus would not dismiss him entirely. That was not his way. He gave the same measure of attention to all who bore the Ventari name.

But his eyes showed less.

Ermerus received less. From less he came to desire more.

Father was present in body. Absent in every other way.
