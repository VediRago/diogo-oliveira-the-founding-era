# Chapter Four: What Came From the Sky

![Rutare Ventari writing his confession](../assets/founding-era-storyboard.jpg)

I was there when they brought him in.

The Servans were the first to see it. Their claim said they were tending the east fields near the lake when the light came. So bright it turned day into night.

I didn't fully understand what I was witnessing. The Servans brought a child wrapped in cloth, and my father got angry. I clearly remember him asking if they were mocking him.

The child could not have been more than eleven or twelve.

He was naked, dirty. But what caught me were his eyes. They were black. Black all the way through.
